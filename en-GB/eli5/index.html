What is Emoty?

It's an app for your Android phone that lets you be creative with emojis. Think of it like a coloring book, but with emojis!

What can you do?

  • Make Patterns: Tell the app what you want, and our robot helper (AI) will make a pattern for you.
  • Use Fun Colors: Pick from lots of cool emoji groups like animals, space, or hearts.
  • It's Safe: We don't look at your stuff. What you make stays on your phone.
  • Share Your Art: Show your friends your cool emoji pictures!
