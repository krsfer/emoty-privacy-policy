Privacy Policy for Emoty

Effective Date: 19th July 2025
Last Updated: 19th July 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Emoty! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our emoji pattern creation app with advanced progression system and AI assistance. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

User-Created Content

  • Emoji Patterns: The emoji sequences and patterns you create
  • Pattern Names: Names you give to your saved patterns (including AI-generated suggestions you choose)
  • Tags and Organization: Tags, favorites, and organizational data you add to patterns
  • Creation Timestamps: When patterns were created or modified

App Usage Information

  • Preferences: Your language setting (English/French), theme preference (light/dark)
  • App State: Current pattern being worked on (saved locally for app continuity)

AI Service Data

  • Emoji Sequences: When you use our AI naming feature, we send your emoji sequences to Anthropic's Claude AI service to generate creative pattern names
  • EmotyBot Conversations: When you chat with the AI assistant EmotyBot (Advanced level), your messages are sent to Claude AI to generate responses
  • Voice Commands: Voice commands are processed locally on your device and converted to app actions (no audio is sent to servers)
  • No Personal Information: We do not send any personal information, device identifiers, or user data to AI services

User Progression Data

  • User Level: Your progression level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert) based on activity
  • Activity Counters: Number of patterns created, active days, features used
  • Reputation Score: Behavioural score for security and access levels
  • Feature Usage: Which features you have discovered and used

Accessibility Data

  • Accessibility Settings: Your preferences for touch gestures, hand size calibration
  • Accessibility Profile: Settings for motor, visual, and cognitive needs
  • Calibration Data: Custom gesture settings for hand size adaptation

How We Use Your Information

Local Storage

  • All your patterns, preferences, and app data are stored locally on your device
  • We use this data to provide app functionality, save your creations, and maintain your preferences
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers

Advanced AI Features

  • Pattern Naming: When you request AI-generated names, we send only your emoji sequence to Claude AI
  • EmotyBot Assistant: Conversations with the AI assistant for creative help, pattern suggestions, and support
  • AI Pattern Generation: Creation of new patterns based on your preferences and history
  • Voice-AI Processing: Complex voice commands may use AI for better understanding
  • No personal information or device identifiers are included in these requests

Progression System

  • Progressive Discovery: The interface adapts to your level to reveal features gradually
  • Access Control: Some advanced features unlock with experience
  • Achievement Tracking: Progression milestones and feature unlocks

Voice Commands

  • Local Processing: Voice recognition occurs on your device (English and French)
  • No Audio Storage: Audio files are not saved or transmitted
  • Text Conversion: Voice is converted to text commands to control the app

App Functionality

  • We use your preferences to customize the app experience (language, theme)
  • Pattern data enables features like search, favorites, import/export, and organization

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All your data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure database systems
  • You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time
  • Data remains on your device unless you explicitly export or share it

Data Transmission

  • Limited to AI naming requests (emoji sequences only)
  • All network communications use secure HTTPS encryption
  • No background data collection or transmission

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Emoty is safe for children under 13 with enhanced protections:

  • No Personal Information Collection: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers
  • No Social Features: No chat, messaging, or social interaction features
  • Local Data Only: All personal data stays on the device under parental control
  • Age-Appropriate Progression: The level system reveals complexity gradually, appropriate to the child's capabilities
  • Safe AI Usage:
    • AI features only process emoji sequences and creative conversations
    • EmotyBot is designed for appropriate educational and creative interactions
    • No personal data in AI requests
  • Secure Voice Commands: Local processing only, no audio transmitted
  • Enhanced Parental Control: Parents can disable AI features via device settings
  • No Advertising: No third-party advertising or tracking

Parents can review and delete all app data through the device's application settings, and control access to advanced features.

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Delete Data: Uninstall the app to remove all data, or use device settings to clear app data
  • Export Data: Use the app's export feature to save your patterns
  • Opt-Out of AI: You can create patterns without using the AI naming feature

App Permissions

  • Internet: Required for AI features (naming, EmotyBot, pattern generation)
  • Microphone: For multilingual voice commands (local processing only)
  • Storage: For saving pattern images to your device gallery (with your permission)
  • Notifications: For app-related notifications and progression achievements (can be disabled)
  • Accessibility: For advanced accessibility features and gesture calibration

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Remains on your device until you delete the app or clear its data
  • AI Requests: Emoji sequences sent to Anthropic are not stored by us (see Anthropic's privacy policy for their practices)

International Users

  • Data processing occurs locally on your device
  • AI requests are processed by Anthropic (see their privacy policy for international data handling)
